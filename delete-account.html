BRAGIflash — Delete Your Account

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the app. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the BRAGIflash app.
  2. Go to the Settings tab (bottom navigation bar).
  3. Tap Account.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile and authentication credentials
  • All vocabulary, collections, and translations
  • Learning progress and practice history
  • School/classroom memberships
  • Analytics and usage data
Note: Deletion is initiated immediately. Residual copies in backups may persist for a limited period consistent with our backup cycles, as described in our Privacy Policy.

Can't access the app?

If you are unable to log in or access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us at bragiflash.privacy@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.
