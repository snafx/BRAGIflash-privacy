BRAGIflash — Delete Local Data

You can erase all locally stored data from your device without deleting your cloud account. This removes everything from the app on your device while keeping your cloud data intact.

Option 1: From within the app

  1. Open the BRAGIflash app.
  2. Go to the Settings tab (bottom navigation bar).
  3. Tap Delete Phone Data.
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

This removes all local vocabulary, collections, progress, and cached school data from your device. Your app preferences (language, theme) are preserved. If you have a cloud account, your data will be re-synced on next login.

Option 2: From device settings

  1. Open your device Settings.
  2. Go to Apps (or Applications).
  3. Find and tap BRAGIflash.
  4. Tap Storage.
  5. Tap Clear Data (or Clear Storage).

This removes all app data including preferences, database, and cached files.

Option 3: Uninstall the app

Uninstalling BRAGIflash removes all locally stored data from your device. If you have a cloud account, your data remains on the server and will be available if you reinstall and log in again.

Want to delete everything, including cloud data? Use the Delete Account option in the app to permanently remove both local and cloud data.
